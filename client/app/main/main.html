What is HackIDC?

HackIDC is Israelâ€™s leading student Hackathon, held annually at IDC Herzliya.
It is a great opportunity to build something new and exciting, together with coding and design enthusiasts.
Students work in teams of up to five people for thirty-six hours to create a web, mobile, or hardware product.

Who can apply?

Any current student at any academic institution, majoring in any subject.
Most accepted participants come with some coding or design background, to make sure they can contribute to the event.
If you believe you are able to contribute in another way, we encourage you to apply.
